Profile

A hands-on technologist and consultant, passionate about building and running technological solutions for real world problems.

I am a developer with a background in and passion for infrastructure, currently working as a DevOps Consultant on an independent contractor basis.

I am an Agile, DevOps and Cloud evangelist. I enjoy team work and working towards a common goal as part of a like-minded and empowered group. I don't like to do anything twice - machines are there to do what we tell them. I think monitoring should be timely, clear and found in the right detail at the right place. I think anything that can be captured as code should be captured as code, including infrastructure - which while I have loved Puppet and Ansible in the past - should preferably be immutable / disposable.

As a technologist I am interested in and curious about anything the comes my way. I am confident with Python and Perl and familiar and happy to work with Java, Ruby and Go. In general I am interested to learn any new language or technology as required!

I see my role as finding ways to use technology to efficiently deploy, test and manage software systems at all levels of the technology stack and through all stages of the software development life-cycle.
